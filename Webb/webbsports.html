The Webb Schools Gauls Varsity Baseball

Baseball is one of the most popular sports at Webb Schools. Webb currently holds nine league championship in baseball since 1931. Webb Schools baseball team the Gauls is currently in Divison 7 in the CIF. The sport encourages teamwork, community, and athlete.

The Webb Schools Cross Country

One of the best sports you can enjoy in the fall, cross country offers a season full of fun and memories. Whether it through running on picturesque mountains or a run to the local coffe shop, it is a sport for everyone. Currently, Webb's cross country WSC hold eight consecutive league championship.
